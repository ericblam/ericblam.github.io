Things the Partnership should Have

□ Shoe Brush
□ Lint Brush
□ Safety Pins
□ Athletic Tape
□ Castor Oil (helps shoes adhere to floor if too slippery)
□ Water Bottle
□ Towel (to wipe sweat)
□ Portable Fan
□ Tylenol, Advil, or Aspirin
□ Scissors
□ Nail Clippers
□ Hot Glue Gun (and glue sticks)
□ Sewing Kit
□ Leatherman or Small Tool Kit
□ Stain Removing Wipes/Marker
□ Comfortable Travelling Clothes
□ Cover-up Sweater, Jacket, or Robe for between rounds

Things the Man should Have

□ Shaving Supplies
□ Hair Products
□ Bronzer (optional)
□ Make up (optional) (foundation, highlighter, powder)
□ Make up Remover (optional)
□ Vaseline (shines and prevents scuffing)
□ Socks & Underwear (+extras)

Man's Latin/Rhythm

□ Latin Pants
□ Latin Shirt (+extra)
□ Latin Shoes
□ Belt (optional)

Men's Syllabus Standard/Smooth

□ Standard/Smooth Pants
□ Standard/Smooth Shirt (+extra)
□ Standard/Smooth Shoes
□ Socks
□ Belt (optional)
□ Vest/ Cardigan
□ Tie or Bowtie
□ Cufflinks (optional)

Men's Open Standard

□ Full Tailsuit (top and bottom)
□ Shirt (+extra)
□ Waistcoat (+extra)
□ Bowtie (+extra)
□ Collar (+extra)
□ Studs, Cufflinks (+extra)
□ Suspenders (+extra)
□ Standard Shoes

Men's Open Smooth

□ Full Suit (top and bottom)
□ Shirt (+extra)
□ Vest (+extra)
□ Tie (+extra)
□ Studs, Cufflinks (+extra)

Things the Lady should Have

□ Shaving Supplies
□ Hair Products
□ Hair Bands/Clips
□ Bobby Pins
□ Hair Net (optional)
□ Bronzer (optional)
□ Make up (foundation, highlighter, blush, powder, lipstick, eye liner, eye shadow, mascara)
□ Make up Remover
□ Fake Nails + Glue or Nail Polish
□ Fake Eyelashes + Glue
□ Jewelry (earrings, necklace, bracelets)
□ Heel Protectors

Lady's Latin/Rhythm

□ Shirt/Skirt or Dress
□ Latin Shoes
□ Fishnet or Pantyhose (optional)
□ Dance Pants
□ Gloves (optional)

Lady's Standard/Smooth

□ Shirt/Skirt or Gown
□ Standard/Smooth Shoes
□ Dance Pants
□ Gloves (optional)
