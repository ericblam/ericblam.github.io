Reverse Top (Silver)

Commence in Contact Position

Man

Step_# Count Steps Position Turn
1 2 RF to side & slightly fwd Contact Up to three complete turns to L over 1-13
2 3 Swivel on Toe of LF to cross in front of RF (Latin Cross) Contact
3 4 RF to side & slightly fwd Contact
4 & Swivel on Toe of LF to cross in front of RF (Latin Cross) Contact
5 1 RF to side & slightly fwd Contact
6 2 Swivel on Toe of LF to cross in front of RF (Latin Cross) Contact
7 3 RF to side & slightly fwd Contact
8 4 Swivel on Toe of LF to cross in front of RF (Latin Cross) Contact
9 & RF to side & slightly fwd Contact
10 1 Swivel on Toe of LF to cross in front of RF (Latin Cross) Contact
11 2 RF to side & slightly fwd Contact
12 3 Swivel on Toe of LF to cross in front of RF (Latin Cross) Contact
13 4&1 Side Chasse, RLR Closed

1) The exact amount of turn made over the course of this figure may vary

Lady

Step_# Count Steps Position Turn
1 2 LF behind RF (Latin Cross) Contact Up to three complete turns to L over 1-13
2 3 RF back & slightly to side Contact
3 4 LF behind RF (Latin Cross) Contact
4 & RF back & slightly to side Contact
5 1 LF behind RF (Latin Cross) Contact
6 2 RF back & slightly to side Contact
7 3 LF behind RF (Latin Cross) Contact
8 4 RF back & slightly to side Contact
9 & LF behind RF (Latin Cross) Contact
10 1 RF back & slightly to side Contact
11 2 LF behind RF (Latin Cross) Contact
12 3 RF back & slightly to side Contact
13-15 4&1 Side Chasse, LRL Closed

1) The exact amount of turn made over the course of this figure may vary
2) When following step 10 with the Aida or the Spiral, turn 5/8 to L on step 10 (Spiral Action)

Preceding Figures

Commenced in Contact Position As described in the charts above
PreBronze 1-5 Closed Basic; 1-5 Time Step
Silver 1-5 Cross Basic
   
Preceding Step 6 Commence the Reverse Top from step 6
PreBronze Closed Basic; Time Step
Bronze Closed Hip Twist
Silver Open Hip Twist; Spiral; Curl; Cross Basic

Following Figures

Ended in Closed Position As described in the charts above
PreBronze Closed Basic; Basic in Place; L Side Shoulder to Shoulder; Hand to Hand to RSP; Side Step to L commenced with LF; Side Step to R commenced with LF; There and Back; Time Step
Bronze Fan
Silver Cross Basic
   
Following Step 10 Having danced only until step 10 of the Reverse Top
Silver Opening Out from Reverse Top; Aida; Spiral
