Spot Turn to Left

Commence in Open PP, with L to R hand hold, R to L hand hold, or without hold

Man

Step_# Count Steps Position Turn
1 2 RF fwd & across to end back & slightly to side RSP to LSP 1/8 to L before 1, then 3/8 at the end of 1 (Fwd Walk Turning)
2 3 Transfer weight to LF LSP  
3-5 4&1 Side Chasse, RLR Closed 3/8 to L between 2-3

1) Step 2 may be danced as "LF fwd, having brushed to RF"
2) May be danced as a part of the Time Step Challenge

Lady (Spot Turn to R)

Step_# Count Steps Position Turn
1 2 LF fwd & across to end back & slightly to side RSP to LSP 1/8 to R before 1, then 3/8 at the end of 1 (Fwd Walk Turning)
2 3 Transfer weight to RF LSP  
3-5 4&1 Side Chasse, LRL Closed 3/8 to R between 2-3

1) Step 2 may be danced as "RF fwd, having brushed to LF"
2) May be danced as a part of the Time Step Challenge

Preceding Figures

Commenced in Open PP As described in the charts above
PreBronze 1-5 Closed Basic; New York to LSP; Spot Turn to R (Lady to L); Underarm Turn to L; L Side Shoulder to Shoulder; Hand to Hand to RSP; Side Steps to L commenced with LF; 1-5 Time Step
Bronze Three Cha Chas in RSP
Silver 1-5 Cross Basic; LF Cuban Break; Split Cuban Break commenced with RF
   
Commenced in RSP Having maintained RSP from the previous figure, making no turn between the last step of the previous figure and step 1 of the Spot Turn
Bronze Three Cha Chas in RSP

Following Figures

Ended in Closed Position As described above in the charts
PreBronze Closed Basic; Basic in Place; L Side Shoulder to Shoulder; Hand to Hand to RSP; Side Step to L commenced with LF; Side Step to R commenced with LF; There and Back; Time Step
Bronze Fan
Silver Cross Basic; Rope Spinning; LF Cuban Break; Split Cuban Break commenced with LF
   
Ended in Open CPP Man turns 1/8 to L over steps 3-5, and the Lady makes 1/8 less turn overall
PreBronze New York to LSP; Spot Turn to R (Lady to L); Underarm Turn to L
Bronze Three Cha Chas in LSP (from step 4)
Silver LF Cuban Break; Split Cuban Break commenced with LF
   
Following Step 2 Having made no turn between 2-3 to maintain LSP
Ended in LSP
Bronze Three Cha Chas in LSP
