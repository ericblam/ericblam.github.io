Commence in Contact Position

Man

Step_# Count Steps Position Turn
1 2 RF to side & slightly fwd Contact 1/8 to L
2 3 Swivel on Toe of LF to cross in front of RF (Latin Cross) Contact 1/8 to L
3-5 4&1 Side Chasse, RLR End in Fan 1/8 to L over 3-5

1) The exact amount of turn made over the course of this figure may vary

Lady

Step_# Count Steps Position Turn
1 2 LF behind RF (Latin Cross) Contact 1/8 to L
2 3 RF back & slightly to side Contact 1/8 to L
3-5 4&1 Back Lock, LRL End in Fan 3/8 to L between 2-3

1) The exact amount of turn made over the course of this figure may vary
