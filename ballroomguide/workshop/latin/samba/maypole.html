Man

Commence in Closed Position, facing Wall
This figure uses Bounce Action

Step_# Steps Turn Alignment Moving
1 LF crosses in front of RF (Latin Cross) 1/4 to L Facing LOD Under Body
2 RF to side, part weight, toe turned out 1/8 to L Pointing DW Varies
3 LF crosses in front of RF (Latin Cross) 1/8 to L Facing Centre Under Body
4 RF to side, part weight, toe turned out 1/8 to L Pointing DC Varies
5 LF crosses in front of RF (Latin Cross) 1/8 to L Facing against LOD Under Body
6 RF to side, part weight, toe turned out 1/8 to L Pointing DC against LOD Varies
7 LF crosses in front of RF (Latin Cross) 1/8 to L Facing Wall Under Body
Step_# Timing Rhythm Position Footwork
1 1 S   BF
2  a a   i/e of T
3 2 S   BF
4 a a   i/e of T
5 1 S   BF
6 a a   i/e of T
7 2 S Closed BF

1) The Man's steps are a Circular Volta
2) This figure may be extended, repeating the Volta action (steps 2-3) for an extra two or four beats
3) If commenced in Open PP, the Man and Lady will both turn 1/8 less throughout
4) If ended in Open CPP, the Man and Lady will both turn 1/8 more throughout

1) The Lady's steps are a Continuous Solo Spot Volta, turning underarm
2) This figure may be extended, repeating the Volta action (steps 2-3) for an extra two or four beats
3) If commenced in Open PP, the Man and Lady will both turn 1/8 less throughout
4) If ended in Open CPP, the Man and Lady will both turn 1/8 more throughout
5) The Lady may make up to one full turn per beat
