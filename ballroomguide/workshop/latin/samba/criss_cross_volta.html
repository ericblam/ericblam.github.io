Criss Cross Voltas (Bronze)

Man

Commence in Open Promenade Position with L to R hand hold, facing DW
This figure uses Bounce Action

Step_# Steps Turn Alignment Moving
1 LF crosses in front of RF (Latin Cross), passing behind Lady 1/8 to L Facing LOD Under Body
2 RF to side & slightly back, part weight, toe turned out   Pointing DW Wall
3 LF crosses in front of RF (Latin Cross) 1/4 to L over 2-3 Facing Centre Under Body
4 RF to side & slightly back, part weight, toe turned out   Pointing LOD LOD
5 LF crosses in front of RF (Latin Cross)   Facing Centre Under Body
6 RF to side & slightly back, part weight, toe turned out   Pointing LOD LOD
7 LF crosses in front of RF (Latin Cross)   Facing Centre Under Body
8 RF crosses in front of LF (Latin Cross), passing behind Lady 1/4 to R Facing LOD Under Body
9 LF to side & slightly back, part weight, toe turned out   Pointing DC Centre
10 RF crosses in front of LF (Latin Cross) 1/4 to R over 9-10 Facing Wall Under Body
11 LF to side & slightly back, part weight, toe turned out   Pointing LOD LOD
12 RF crosses in front of LF (Latin Cross)   Facing Wall Under Body
13 LF to side & slightly back, part weight, toe turned out   Pointing LOD LOD
14 RF crosses in front of LF (Latin Cross)   Facing Wall Under Body

1) On steps 3-7 the RF points LOD, the LF points to Centre, and the body faces DC; on steps 10-14 the LF point LOD, the RF points to Wall, and the body faces DW
2) This figure may be extended, repeating the Volta action for an extra two or four beats after step 7 and/or after step 14
3) Steps 6-7 may be replaced by steps 2-3 of a LF Bota Fogo; steps 13-14 may be replaced with steps 2-3 of a RF Bota Fogo
4) (Gold) The first step may be held for an additional beat, with a strong upward stretch before the step and a dropping of energy on the step itself, known as the Dropped Volta (this may be done twice in a row); this may also be used on the eighth step
