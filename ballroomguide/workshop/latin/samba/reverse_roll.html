Reverse Roll (Gold)

Man

Commence in Closed Position with Closed Hold, Facing LOD
This figure does not use Bounce Action

Step_# Steps Turn Alignment Moving
1 LF fwd 1/8 to L Facing DC DC
2 RF to side & slightly back, part weight, toe turned out 1/4 to L between 1-2 Backing DW DC
3 RF crosses in front of LF (Latin Cross) 1/8 to L Backing LOD Under Body
4 RF back 1/8 to L Backing DC DC
5 LF to side, small step, part weight 3/8 to L between 4-5, body turns less Pointing LOD DC
6 RF closes to LF Body completes turn Facing LOD Under Body
Step_# Timing Rhythm Position Footwork
1 1 S Closed BF
2 2 Q Closed T
3 & Q Closed BF
4 1 S Closed BF
5 2 Q Closed B
6 & Q Closed BF

1) On step 1, send hips back behind body; over steps 2-3, send hips to R and sway to L; on step 4, send hips forward; over step 5-6, send hips to L and sway to R

1 1 S Closed BF
2  2 Q Closed B
