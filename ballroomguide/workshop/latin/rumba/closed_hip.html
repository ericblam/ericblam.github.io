Closed Hip Twist (Bronze)

Commence in Closed Position with Closed Hold, toward partner's R side

Man

Step_# Count Steps Position Turn
1 2 LF fwd (Checked Fwd Walk) RSP 1/8 to R before 1
2 3 Transfer weight to RF RSP  
3 41 RF back (Pressed Back Walk) OP  
4 2 RF back    
5 3 Transfer weight to LF    
6 41 RF to side Fan 1/8 to L

1) The Man's steps are similar to those of the Spiral
2) Step 1-2 may be danced as steps 1-2 of a LF Cucaracha

Lady

Step_# Count Steps Position Turn
1 2 RF back RSP 3/8 to R (Fwd Walk Turning), then an additional 1/4 on LF before 1
2 3 Transfer weight to LF RSP  
3 41 RF fwd & across toward Man's R side (Pressed Fwd Walk) OP 1/2 to L between 2-3
4 2 LF fwd   1/4 to R between 3-4
5 3 RF fwd to end back & slightly to side   3/8 to L (Fwd Walk Turning)
6 41 LF back Fan 1/4 to L between 5-6

1) The Lady's steps are similar to those of the Spiral

Preceding Figures

Commenced in Closed Position toward Partner's R Side As described in the charts above
Bronze Alemana; Natural Top; Opening Out to R and L
Silver Rope Spinning
Gold Three Threes; Three Alemanas; Continuous Hip Twist
   
Commenced in Shadow Position Lady omits the Fwd Walk Turning before step 1
Gold Sliding Doors

Following Figures

Ended in Fan Position As described in the charts above
Bronze Alemana; Hockey Stick
Gold Sliding Doors; Fencing; Three Alemanas
   
Ended in Open Position Man turns 1/4 to L between steps 5-6 and steps RF fwd on 6
PreBronze Open Basic; Progressive Walks Forward; L Side Shoulder to Shoulder
Bronze Alemana
Silver Open Hip Twist; Curl
Gold Three Alemanas; Three Threes
   
Ended in Open CPP Man and Lady turn an additional 1/8 to L between steps 5-6, stepping diag fwd on step 6
PreBronze New York to LSP; Spot Turn to R (Lady L); Underarm Turn to L
   
Ended in Contact Position Man turns 3/8 to L over steps 5-6 to end RF to side & slightly fwd. Lady turns an additional 1/8 between steps 5-6 to end LF behind RF (Latin Cross)
Silver 4-9 Reverse Top
