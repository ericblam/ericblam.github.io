Left Whisk (Gold)

Man

Commence in Closed Position, backing LOD

Step_# Steps Turn Alignment Moving
1 RF back Com to turn L Backing LOD LOD
2 LF to side & slightly fwd 3/8 between 1-2, body turns less Pointing DW LOD
3 RF crosses behind LF Body completes turn Facing DW Under Body
4 Twist to the R on both feet   Toward the alignment of step 7  
5 Continue to twist   Toward the alignment of step 7  
6 Continue to twist   Toward the alignment of step 7  
7 End with weight on RF Gradually turn 1 full turn to the R over 4-7, body turns more Backing DC against LOD  
Step_# Timing Position Rise & Fall Sway Footwork
1 1 Closed   TH
2  2 Closed   R TH
3 3 CPP   R T
4 1 CPP   Flat (LF), T (RF)
5 & Closed   Flat (LF), T (RF)
6 2 OP   Flat (LF), T (RF)
7 3 Closed   TH

The following step will be taken back in CBMP with the Lady outside

1) The Left Whisk may be commenced in PP moving down LOD, step 1 taken as RF fwd & across in CBMP, HT
2) If the Left Whisk is commenced in PP, it may be commenced from step 3, immediately crossing RF behind LF (count 1), continuing with steps 4-7 (count 2&3&)
3) If the Left Whisk is commenced in PP, it may be commenced from step 3, immediately crossing RF behind LF (count 1), replacing weight to LF (count 2) and stepping RF to side facing Wall (count 3), and then omitting steps 4-7 to follow instead with a Contra Check

Lady

Commence in Closed Position, facing LOD

Step_# Steps Turn Alignment Moving
1 LF fwd Com to turn L Facing DC LOD
2 RF to side & slightly back 3/8 between 1-2 Backing DW LOD
3 LF crosses in front of RF 1/8 between 2-3 Backing LOD Under Body
4 RF fwd, small step Com to turn R Facing against LOD Against LOD
5 LF fwd, small step 3/8 between 4-5 Facing DC Under Body
6 RF fwd, small step 3/8 between 5-6 Facing Wall Under Body
7 LF fwd, small step 3/8 between 6-7 Facing DC against LOD Under Body
Step_# Timing Position Rise & Fall Sway Footwork
1 1 Closed   HT
2  2 Closed   L T
3 3 CPP   L TH
4 1 CPP   HT
5 & Closed   T
6 2 OP   T
7 3 Closed   TH

The following step will be taken forward in CBMP, OP

1) The Left Whisk may be commenced in PP moving down LOD, step 1 taken as LF fwd & across in CBMP and turning only 1/4 between steps 1-2
2) If the Left Whisk is commenced in PP, it may be commenced from step 3, immediately crossing the LF in front of the RF (count 1), continuing with steps 4-7 (count 2&3&)
3) If the Left Whisk is commenced in PP, it may be commenced from step 3, immediately crossing the LF in front of the RF (count 1), replacing weight to the RF (count 2), and stepping LF to side facing Centre (count 3), and then omitting steps 4-7 to follow instead with a Contra Check
