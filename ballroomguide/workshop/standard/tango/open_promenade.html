Open Promenade (Bronze)

Man

Commence in Promenade Position, feet facing DW, body facing Wall

Step_# Steps Turn Alignment Moving
1 LF to side   Facing DW LOD
2 RF fwd & across in CBMP   Facing DW LOD
3 LF to side & slightly fwd   Facing DW LOD
4 RF fwd in CBMP   Facing DW DW
Step_# Timing Rhythm Position Footwork
1 12 S PP HF
2 3 Q PP HF
3 4 Q Closed i/e of WF
4 56 S OP WF

1) This figure is like a Closed Promenade with an Open Finish

Lady

Commence in Promenade Position, feet facing DC, body facing Centre

Step_# Steps Turn Alignment Moving
1 RF to side   Facing DC LOD
2 LF fwd & across in CBMP   Facing DC LOD
3 RF to side & slightly back 1/4 to L between 2-3, body turns less Backing DW LOD
4 LF back in CBMP   Backing DW DW
Step_# Timing Rhythm Position Footwork
1 12 S PP HF
2 3 Q PP HF
3 4 Q Closed i/e of BH
4 56 S OP WF

1) This figure is like a Closed Promenade with an Open Finish
