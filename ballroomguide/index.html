  • About Ballroom Guide

    We love Ballroom and Latin Dance, and believe to get better you must not only train hard, but train smart. That's it is our goal to be a guide for beginner, intermediate, and advanced dancers to every resource a Ballroom Dancer could want. Ballroom Guide offers articles on fitness for dancers, a beginner's guide to competition, reviews on dance shoe brands, dance attire, the best training apps, as well as every move in for International Ballroom and Latin Dancing, covering Waltz, Tango, Slow Foxtrot, Viennese Waltz, Quickstep, Cha Cha, Samba, Rumba, Paso Doble, and Jive! We also provide the technical charts for all the figures in the Syllabus in written form, and some in video - 100% free.
